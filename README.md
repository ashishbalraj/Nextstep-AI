📘 NextStep AI
AI-Powered Career Guidance for Students

NextStep AI is an intelligent career counselor designed to help students discover the right career path based on their academic background, interests, and personality traits.
The platform uses NLP, machine learning, and an interactive chatbot to guide students with personalized suggestions.

🚀 Features (MVP)
🔮 1. Career Recommendation Engine

Combines academic details, interests, and essay-based personality traits

Predicts suitable career domains (e.g., Data Science, Engineering, Design, etc.)


💼 2. Job Suggestions

Provides job titles

Skills required

Average industry salaries

Growth and demand insights

🤖 4. Interactive Chatbot

Students can ask:

“What skills do I need for Data Science?”
“Is UI/UX design suitable for me?”
“What jobs can I get after Computer Engineering?”
